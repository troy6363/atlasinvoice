FAQ
Common Questions
Everything you need to know before booking your luxury ride.
How do I book a ride?
Booking is simple — use the form on this page, call us directly at 901-249-9476, or send a text message.
              We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for guaranteed availability, though we accommodate
              last-minute requests whenever possible.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the entire Memphis metropolitan area including Germantown, Collierville, Bartlett, East Memphis,
              Downtown, Midtown, as well as Southaven and Olive Branch in Mississippi, and West Memphis, Arkansas.
              Long-distance trips are available upon request.
How is pricing determined?
Pricing depends on the type of service, distance, and duration. We provide transparent quotes before you
              book — no hidden fees, no surge pricing. Contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your itinerary.
Are your drivers background-checked?
Yes. All She Delivers chauffeurs undergo thorough background checks and are professionally trained. We
              are fully licensed and insured, and your safety and comfort are our highest priorities.
What is your cancellation policy?
We understand plans change. Cancellations made at least 12 hours before your scheduled pickup are fully
              refunded. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to modify or cancel a reservation.
Do you track flights for airport pickups?
Absolutely. We monitor your flight status in real time and adjust your pickup time automatically. Whether
              your flight is early, on time, or delayed, your chauffeur will be ready when you land.
